How the portal works

1 📋
Screen the tenant
Fill the KYT Pre-Rental Questionnaire. Generate a compressed share link and send to the applicant via WhatsApp, Email, or SMS.
2 📤
Collect consent
Send consent links to referees, guarantors, witness, and agent directly from the form. Each receives a role-specific legal advisory before signing.
3 📝
Execute agreement
Complete the Tenancy Agreement e-form. All parties sign digitally. Clause X obligation to vacate is formally acknowledged.
4 🔖
Manage deviations
Use the Tenancy Deviation Requisition for mid-tenancy requests. 3-step workflow — landlord sets terms, tenant fills, landlord approves.
