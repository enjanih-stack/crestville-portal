How It Works

From enquiry to key handover — a clear, structured, professional process.

1 🔍
Browse & Choose
View all available units with amenities, location details, and expected availability date. Filter by city or property type.
2 📋
Complete KYT Form
Fill the Know Your Tenant questionnaire. Upload your passport photo, NIN, and any supporting documents directly in the form.
3 👥
Referee & Guarantor
Enter your referees and guarantors. They receive an email or WhatsApp link with a legal advisory — they sign digitally and a confirmation code is sent to the landlord.
4 📝
Sign Agreement
Once approved, the full tenancy agreement is sent digitally. All parties sign — landlord, tenant, guarantors, and witness — with Clause X formally acknowledged.
5 🔑
Get Your Keys
Electricity meter details are recorded, estate dues explained, and keys handed over. Your portal account gives you access to all your documents.
