3 Conditions & Compliance
Clause 3.1 — No Unauthorised AlterationsNo permanent alterations, penetrations, drilling, or modifications to the building shall be made without the written approval granted by this form. Any deviation from the described scope requires a fresh requisition.
Clause 3.2 — Regulatory ComplianceAll works must comply with applicable Nigerian Building Codes, NERC standards (electrical), and all relevant local authority regulations. Specialist works must be carried out by licensed certified tradespeople.
Clause 3.3 — Reinstatement ObligationUnless otherwise agreed in writing, the Tenant shall restore all affected areas to original condition at end of tenancy at the Tenant's cost.
Clause 3.4 — Full Tenant LiabilityThe Tenant accepts full and sole liability for all damage to the building, fixtures, common areas, persons, or animals arising from the approved deviation works, during and after tenancy.
